In the Spotlight

Keith Timmins

This quarter's Client Spotlight goes to Keith Timmins. He has inspired us with his commitment to well-being. Keith took a break from work and running to give us a glimpse into his passion.

How did your passion for running come about?

My main "track" experience in junior high and high school was pole vaulting . . . a very short sprint carry-ing a long pole! I enlisted in the U.S. Marine Corps during my senior year in high school and shipped off to boot-camp a few weeks after graduating. On our 2nd day there, we had a "PFT" (Personal Fitness Test), which included a 3-mile run - further than I had never run before. I did the 3-miles under 24 minutes, was 6th out of the 80 guys in my platoon and I did not die!
